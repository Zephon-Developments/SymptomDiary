# Symptom Diary

A patient-focused symptom tracking application designed for individuals managing long-term conditions.

## Purpose

Symptom Diary helps patients with chronic or long-term conditions maintain a detailed, structured record of their symptoms over time. The primary goal is to empower patients to present clear, evidence-based accounts of their health to consultants, GPs, and other healthcare professionals — improving the quality and efficiency of clinical appointments.

## Who Is It For?

- Patients with chronic illnesses, complex conditions, or ongoing undiagnosed symptoms
- Anyone who needs to track patterns in their health over weeks, months, or years
- Patients preparing for specialist consultations who need to present a timeline of symptoms
- People whose conditions fluctuate and who struggle to recall details accurately during appointments

## Key Goals

- **Evidence for Consultants & GPs** — Provide exportable, readable summaries of symptom history that patients can share directly with healthcare professionals
- **Pattern Recognition** — Help patients and clinicians identify trends, triggers, and correlations across symptoms over time
- **Accurate Recall** — Remove reliance on memory during appointments by maintaining a timestamped log of how the patient has been feeling day-to-day
- **Accessible Reporting** — Generate clear, human-readable reports suitable for clinical review
